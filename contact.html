Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need my own equipment?
No! We offer high-quality recurve and compound bow rentals for all ages and sizes. First-time rentals include arm guards and finger tabs.
Is there an age limit?
We welcome archers aged 8 and up. Minors under 16 must be accompanied by a guardian. We have specific youth leagues and lightweight bows for younger archers.
Do you offer private lessons?
Yes, we have certified Level 3 & 4 coaches available for private 1-on-1 sessions. Rates start at $60/hr. Book via the Services page or call us.
