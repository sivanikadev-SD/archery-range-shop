League Rules & Scoring

How does scoring work?
We use standard World Archery indoor scoring. The center gold ring is 10 points, moving outward to 1 point. X-ring hits are used for tie-breakers. Scorecards must be double-signed.
What equipment is allowed?
Specific leagues have equipment classes (Open, Bowhunter, Barebow). Generally, max draw weight is 60lbs for indoor leagues to protect our targets. Carbon and aluminum arrows are permitted.
Can I make up a missed week?
Yes! You can shoot a make-up score during any open practice time within 1 week of the missed session. Just inform the front desk before you start scoring.
