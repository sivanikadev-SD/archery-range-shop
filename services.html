Range Safety Rules

  • 01.

    Never point a bow at anything you do not intend to shoot. Arrows must only be nocked at the shooting line.

  • 02.

    Wait for the "All Clear" whistle command before retrieving arrows.

  • 03.

    Inspect your equipment before every session. Damaged arrows or strings can be dangerous.

  • 04.

    Wear appropriate clothing. No open-toed shoes or loose hanging jewelry/straps.
